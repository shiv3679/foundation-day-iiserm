About Stitch a Story

Aliens are at your doorstep, what do you do? Found a peculiar egg, what would it unfold? A story started by your own imagination with an ending unknown to you. Stitch a story with your friend and win exciting prizes! Students in class 6 and above are welcome to come write a story with us.
